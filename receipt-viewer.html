Public-Safe Audit Receipt Viewer

This viewer shows what a public-safe receipt proves at the workflow boundary, what it does not prove, and how the two Phase 1 terminal outcomes differ.

What The Sample Proves

  • the execution reached a terminal outcome
  • the receipt records the contract boundary and validation result
  • delivery state is reported separately from outcome truth

What It Does Not Prove

  • raw inputs, prompts, or internal routing details
  • any hidden audit events beyond the public-safe receipt scope
  • that a public receipt alone exposes the full internal audit trail

succeeded and failed_safe are both terminal outcomes with different meanings: one confirms validated success, and the other confirms the workflow stopped safely without claiming success.
